About Us


Desi Matka Ltd.
Welcome to Desi Matka, your one-stop destination for beautifully crafted clay pots that bring a touch of tradition and elegance to your home. Desi Matka is dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of clay pottery, a timeless craft that has been an integral part of our culture for centuries. Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring authentic, handcrafted clay pots to people who appreciate the beauty and utility of this traditional craft. Each piece in our collection is a testament to the skill and dedication of our artisans.


Vision


Our vision at Desi Matka is to become a leading provider of high-quality, handcrafted clay pots while promoting the rich cultural heritage of pottery. We aspire to create a world where traditional crafts are valued and preserved, and where artisans are recognized and rewarded for their exceptional skills. We aim to inspire a return to sustainable and eco-friendly living through the use of our authentic clay products.

Mission


Our mission is to promote sustainable living and support local craftsmanship by providing high-quality, authentic clay pots. We are committed to:
  • Authenticity: Ensuring each product is 100% handcrafted using traditional techniques.
  • Quality: Using the finest natural materials for durability and safety.
  • Sustainability: Offering eco-friendly products that promote sustainable living.
    • Cultural Connection: Reflecting the rich cultural heritage and artistry of our skilled artisans in each piece.

Goals

    Preserve Traditional Craftsmanship: To ensure the continuation of traditional clay pottery techniques by supporting and empowering local artisans.
  • Promote Sustainable Living: To encourage the use of eco-friendly clay products that are safe for the environment and beneficial for health.
  • Expand Our Reach: To grow our customer base and make our products accessible to a broader audience, both locally and internationally.
  • Enhance Customer Experience: To provide exceptional customer service and continually improve our product offerings based on customer feedback and needs.
  • Support Artisan Communities: To improve the livelihoods of our artisans by providing fair wages, safe working conditions, and opportunities for growth and development.
